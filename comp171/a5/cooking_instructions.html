--- Directions ---
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
2. Sautee chicken on stove with
vegetable oil
3. Sautee mushrooms and zuchini on
stove with vegetable oil
4. Sautee black beans on stove
5. Mix chicken, mushrooms, zuchini,
salsa, and black beans in a bowl
6. Wrap mixed ingredients in
tortillas
7. Sprinkle cheese on top of the
toritllas
8. Cook in oven for 25 minutes
9. Let sit for 5 minutes to cool
