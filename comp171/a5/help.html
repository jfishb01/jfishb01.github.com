Dine on a Dime

  • Dine on a Dime provides you with
    a weekly list of meals and ingredients
    based on your preferences.
  • Enter your preferences by pressing
    the "Set Preferences" button.
  • To get a weekly list of groceries and
    recipes, press the "Generate New List" button.
  • View saved lists by pressing the "View Saved Lists" button.
  • Press Back to go back to
    the previous menu at any point.
